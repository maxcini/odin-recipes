Description

Lasagna is a classic italian dish primarily made of pasta, cheese, and meat. It is typically served as a first course, after the appetizer and before the second (main) course, which is usually your meat and veggies.

One cautionary note about Lasagna - it is a heavy pasta dish. So while it tastes amazing, remember to pace yourself if you're eating a multi-course dinner. The italians know how to stuff you!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture
  3. Boil the noodles
  4. Assemble the lasagna (layering)
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and serve
