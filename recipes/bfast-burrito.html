Breakfast Burrito Recipe

Home

Description

Breakfast burritos are the busy person who values being healthy ultimate breakfast hack.

Never skip the most important meal of the day again! Grab a burrito from the freezer, heat it up in the microwave for a few mins, and eat it during your commute to work. Fast, easy, and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Scrambled eggs
  • Cheese
  • Potatoes
  • Chorizo
  • Flour tortrilla
  • Cholula hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Roast veggies
  3. Bake meat source
  4. Bake eggs & cheese
  5. Cut into slices
  6. Roll into tortilla
  7. Wrap in parchment paper & freeze
  8. Microwave when ready to chow
