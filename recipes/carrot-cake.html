Carrot Cake Recipe

Home

Description

Carrot cake is one of my favorite desserts ever. There is something special about the fluffy cake inside paired with the cream cheese frosting.

Funnily enough, one of the best carrot cakes I've had was at my college dorm, Mary Markley. Good times and good cake!

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Grate carrots
  3. Mix dry & wet ingredients
  4. Combine ingredients
  5. Fold in extras
  6. Bake
  7. Cool and frost
